ATMAN Invest Casting

Read the flow embedded in data,
then turn it into an executable scenario.

ATMAN Invest Casting is not a system for simply finding the strongest stocks. It analyzes price and capital flow, a stock’s role within its sector, recurring behavioral traits, current risk, and remaining opportunity space. It then determines what role the stock can play now and connects it to an executable scenario only when the conditions are aligned.

The objective is not a single-point forecast, but prepared response. Rather than committing to one direction, ATMAN prepares three paths: reaching the objective, taking longer than expected, or invalidating the original thesis.

1. How a stock becomes an execution candidate

Only stocks that pass multiple layers of observation and validation are connected to an executable scenario.

01

Multi-angle observation

The same stock is examined through four perspectives: sector, pattern, relationships, and capital concentration.

No conclusion depends on a single indicator or a single point of view.
02

Stock behavior model

Repeated patterns of success, failure, and recovery are accumulated into a living behavioral profile.

Each stock’s distinct character and conditions are remembered separately.
03

Environment-fit candidates

Market and sector conditions are checked first, and only stocks worth examining now enter the candidate pool.

Not every stock is evaluated by the same standard every day.
04

Discover the current role

Historical behavior models are compared with today’s price and capital-flow structure to identify the role the stock can play now.

The role is discovered from current data rather than assigned in advance.
05

Scenario contract

Entry conditions, expected objective, invalidation point, and valid time window are assembled into one execution scenario.

ATMAN selects executable stocks, not merely attractive-looking ones.
06

Rule-based execution

Orders are placed only when real-time data satisfies the agreed conditions. Objectives, stops, and time expiry are then handled by rule.

Action follows predefined conditions, not emotion.

2. Four lenses that make ATMAN different

The same dataset is read through four specialist perspectives and integrated into one stock behavior model.

S

Sector Lens

Determines whether the sector is alive, whether capital is spreading, and whether the stock is acting as a leader, follower, or supporting player.

P

Pattern Lens

Observes the shape of price movement, how long it persists, and how it tends to fail or recover.

T

Tensor Lens

Reads which moves first—price or capital—whether they align, collide, transmit, or lose their relationship.

C

Concentration Lens

Examines where capital is compressed or overheated, whether pressure persists or fades, and how much time may be needed for release.

3. We look for a wave that can be ridden now—not simply a strong stock

ATMAN focuses on how a small change is transmitted into a larger flow, rather than reducing the market to up or down.

Does capital hold even when price pauses?

What matters is not the initial surge itself, but whether higher-order capital flow supports the new price space during the pause that follows.

01
Change in the smaller waveHas a meaningful move begun beyond the previous range?
02
Holding the new spaceDoes the newly created price space remain intact even during a pullback?
03
Capital handoffWhen the shorter move cools, does the higher-order capital flow take over and support the space?
04
Alignment with the larger flowIs the intermediate change linking into the larger wave or beginning to alter the prior structure?

The stock’s current role

Stocks are not traded with the same horizon or response logic. The valid cycle and management style change according to the role revealed by current data.

Short-wave reactionOnly the smaller wave is alive, while confirmation from higher-order flows remains limited.
Intermediate transitionA smaller-wave change is beginning to transfer into intermediate capital flow.
Long-wave trendShort, intermediate, and long flows are connected and the trend is progressing.
Counter-trend recoveryThe larger flow remains weak, but recovery strength is appearing in smaller and intermediate waves.
A role is an analytical output, not an input label. The four labels above are simplified explanations for external readers. In the actual ATMAN system, a role is defined as the wave being ridden + valid cycle N, for example Swing Short Wave (10) · Swing Medium Wave (6) · Swing Long Wave (4).

4. The audition is not a search for “good stocks”

Even a high-quality stock does not pass if it has already moved too far, offers too little remaining space relative to risk, or lacks clear execution boundaries.

Current similarity

Are its historical strengths still alive?

ATMAN checks whether the behavioral traits stored in the stock model still resemble today’s price and capital-flow structure.

Remaining space

Is the opportunity already too late?

Even if the trend looks strong, the stock is excluded when little space remains before the expected objective.

Asymmetry

Is the reward-to-risk structure favorable?

Expected objective, invalidation, and time uncertainty are evaluated together rather than collapsed into a single score.

Executability

Can the conditions be defined clearly?

A stock does not proceed to execution if entry, objective, exit, and valid time boundaries cannot be specified.

5. Three response paths—not one prediction

Once execution begins, the outcome is managed through three distinct paths.

A

Objective reached

If the expected price structure is reached within the valid time window, the objective path is completed.

B

Time expiry

If neither the objective nor invalidation occurs before time expires, the contract is not extended automatically; it is reviewed or closed.

C

Thesis invalidated

If the price-and-capital conditions supporting the thesis break first, the loss is accepted and the contract is closed.

6. AI interpretation and system execution are separated

AI interprets relationships and meaning. The execution engine verifies predefined conditions without emotion.

What AI does

AI reads complex data relationships and designs the stock’s current role and execution scenario.

Behavior-model comparisonCompare recurring historical traits with the current flow
Price–capital interpretationAssess lead–lag, alignment, collision, and transmission
Current-role discoveryDetermine the active wave and its valid cycle
Scenario designCreate entry, objective, invalidation, and time boundaries

What the execution engine does

The system monitors real-time price and capital data and acts only when the scenario conditions are satisfied.

Condition monitoringContinuously verify the confirmed price-and-capital structure
Entry executionPlace an order only when predefined entry conditions are met
Position managementTrack objectives, warnings, invalidation, and time boundaries
Exit and reviewClose or reassess on objective, stop, or time expiry
The purpose of ATMAN Invest Casting Not to trade more often, but to select only stocks that have been sufficiently observed, can be clearly explained, and still offer a viable reward-to-risk structure—then act according to a scenario defined in advance.
